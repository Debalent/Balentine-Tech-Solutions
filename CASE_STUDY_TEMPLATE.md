## Pattern Recognition

**After collecting 3-5 case studies, answer these questions:**

1. **Common Pain Patterns:**  
   - What operational challenges appear across multiple studios?

2. **Tool Gaps:**  
   - Which existing tools consistently fail? Why?

3. **Pricing Validation:**  
   - What price range appears acceptable across studios?

4. **Emotional Triggers:**  
   - What phrases/feelings recur? (These become marketing copy)

5. **Market Segmentation:**  
   - Are certain studio types (size, location, focus) better fits?

6. **Product Priorities:**  
   - Which features would solve 80% of pain for 80% of studios?
